01

What is AI Trend Radar?

AI Trend Radar is an advanced analytics tool that helps content creators, marketers, and entrepreneurs discover viral topics before they peak. It identifies massive growth opportunities in various niches to help you craft highly engaging content.

02

How does the trend scanner work?

Our proprietary tool aggregates real-time search volume, social media engagement velocity, and niche competition. By analyzing this data, it generates a unique "Opportunity Score" and estimates the growth potential of any given topic over the next 7 to 30 days.

03

Is AI Trend Radar free?

Yes! The core trend scanning features and the basic AI Script Generator are completely free for creators to use. We believe in empowering creators without massive paywalls.

04

Who can use this tool?

Our platform is designed for a wide variety of digital professionals:

• YouTubers & TikTokers looking for viral video ideas.
• Digital Marketers researching hot niches for ad campaigns.
• Bloggers & Copywriters seeking high-traffic article topics.
• SaaS Founders validating new business ideas.
